Tools for Business Owners

These are tools to help you make stronger business decisions.

Margin Calculator

The margin calculator is for figuring out how much money from each sale of a single item you keep given all your costs. It will also tell you how many units of the item you must sell to break even.

Margin Calculator

Bundle Margin Calculator

This tool does the same thing as the first one, but it does it for two items so you can analyze your bundle discount strategy.
