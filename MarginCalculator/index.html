Margin Calculator

A quick "How-To" on using this:

Your fixed costs are things that don't change. For example your rent, table fee, etc. This is the sum of all those things. Rent + Insurance + whatever...

Your variable costs are what it costs you to produce one unit of the thing you are selling.

You margin is the price of the item minus your variable cost.
